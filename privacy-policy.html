Glyppo App – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use Glyppo and explains your privacy rights.

Glyppo is designed with privacy in mind. Any content you create—such as dose logs, weight tracking, symptom records, and medication schedules—is stored locally on your device by default and is never shared with any third party service by Glyppo without your explicit action.

Summary

Your health data and medication information are private. We do not sell your data. We do not monetize through ads. The app is funded through optional paid subscriptions that unlock additional features, so you are never the product. Your data remains yours and stays on your device unless you choose to share or back it up using a service you select.

Collection of Personal Information

Glyppo may allow you to optionally provide personal details to enhance your experience, such as:

  • Medication names, doses, and injection sites
  • Weight measurements and goal tracking data
  • Symptom severity ratings and notes
  • Your preferred reminder settings and schedule preferences
  • App theme and display preferences

This information is stored solely within the app to provide its core functionality. It is not transmitted to Glyppo servers or any external party unless you choose to use a sync or backup feature.

Use of Personal Information

The personal information you provide is used exclusively to deliver and improve Glyppo's features, such as:

  • Tracking your medication schedule and adherence
  • Monitoring weight progress and trends over time
  • Providing timely dose reminders and notifications
  • Generating insights and progress reports for your healthcare team

Analytics

Glyppo may use privacy-friendly, minimal analytics to understand general app usage patterns—such as which screens are most visited—without collecting personally identifiable information or the contents of your health data. No sensitive data is sent to analytics services.

Security of Your Personal Data

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. iOS provides file-level encryption by default. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to Other Websites

Glyppo may include links to third-party sites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as Glyppo evolves. The "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent changes. Significant updates will be posted within the app and on our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:

  • Through the support section in the app's Settings
